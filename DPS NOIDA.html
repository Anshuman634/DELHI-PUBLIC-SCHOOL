Exchange Programs

DPS Noida has an unprecedented six student exchange programs, running concurrently, with schools of Germany, Spain, France, Denmark, Australia and Switzerland. The oldest programme, the Indo German student exchange programme, has entered its eleventh year and has the strong support of Max Muellar Bhawan, Goethe Institute, India. Each year approximately 80-100 students opt for these programmes and visit foreign shores to get an insight into the cultures and customs of their global counterparts.
